The Art Of War

The Art of War" is a timeless treatise on strategy and warfare attributed to Sun Tzu, an ancient Chinese military strategist and philosopher. Written over 2,500 years ago, this seminal work remains profoundly relevant not only in the context of military tactics, but also in various aspects of life, including business, politics, and even personal development.

Who is General Sun Tzu?

Perfect Army Strategist

Sun Tzu, also known as Sunzi, was an ancient Chinese military strategist, philosopher, and author of "The Art of War" . He is believed to have lived during the Eastern Zhou period of ancient China, which is traditionally dated from the 6th century BCE to the 3rd century BCE.
